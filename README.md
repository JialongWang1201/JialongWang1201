# Jialong Wang

M.Eng. Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Ottawa.

My work is mainly in embedded systems, firmware bring-up, RTOS-based software, and low-level debugging. I have completed three internships at Apple across firmware, system validation, and software engineering roles.

## Focus Areas

- Embedded firmware in `C` and `C++`
- `STM32` and `ARM Cortex-M` platforms
- `FreeRTOS`, MPU, and low-level driver work
- Bring-up, debugging, and validation tooling
- Systems-oriented Python tooling for automation and analysis

## Selected Projects

- [TahoeOS](https://github.com/JialongWang1201/TahoeOS): Open-source smartwatch platform based on `STM32F411`, `FreeRTOS`, and `LVGL`
- [MicroKernel-MPU](https://github.com/JialongWang1201/MicroKernel-MPU): `STM32F446` platform focused on staged bring-up, fault isolation, and evidence-first triage
- [Human-Angle-Detector](https://github.com/JialongWang1201/Human-Angle-Detector): Real-time pose and angle tracking with `MediaPipe` and `OpenCV`
